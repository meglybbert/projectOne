My favorite book:

Jane Eyre is my favorite book because it illustrates the life of a young girl, and describes the way she grows up. Time after time, she is presented with decisions that truly would only affect her on a personal level for she is accountable to no one but herself. These decisions raise a lot of moral issues, an Jane manages to keep her morality intact. I've always looked up to the way that Jane stuck to what she believed and what was right, even when no one else was watching her.
